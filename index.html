MEET THE CHARACTERS!

Scroll down to meet everyone!

BARRY

THE HERO

Barry is the CHILLEST capybara you'll ever meet. Nothing fazes him... except maybe evil cupcakes trying to destroy the world. When danger calls, Barry answers with a relaxed sigh and saves the day anyway. He's the hero we all need - calm, cool, and surprisingly good at defeating baked goods.

NOODLE

BARRY'S TANGLED FRIEND

Noodle is a really, REALLY long snake. So long that he's always getting himself tangled up in knots! He means well and tries to help Barry, but usually ends up tied around a tree or wrapped around himself. Despite his tangly troubles, Noodle is a loyal friend who never gives up!

POPSICLE

THE THREE-HAT PENGUIN

Popsicle LOVES hats. One hat? Not enough. Two hats? Still not enough! THREE hats stacked on top of each other? PERFECT! Nobody knows why Popsicle wears three hats, and honestly, nobody asks anymore. He's Barry's loyal friend who always shows up ready to help - with all three hats firmly in place!

TURBO

THE "FASTEST" SNAIL ALIVE

Turbo is 100% CONVINCED he is the fastest creature in the entire world. He's not. He's a snail. But don't tell HIM that! Turbo has a serious hero complex and is always trying to race to save the day. The others usually have to wait for him to catch up, but his enthusiasm is unstoppable!

PROFESSOR FOXY

THE GENIUS INVENTOR

Professor Foxy is the brains of the operation! This clever fox invents all sorts of amazing gadgets to help Barry defeat the baddies. Need a cupcake-deflector? She's got one! Anti-evil-frosting spray? Already invented! She works in her secret lab and always has just the right invention ready when Barry needs it most.

COMMANDER ZOOM

THE VILLAIN

Commander Zoom is a hummingbird with BIG evil plans! He can fly faster than anyone and he ALWAYS wants to take over the world. This time, his plan involves an army of living cupcakes! He wears a fancy commander's hat and thinks he's the greatest villain ever. Will Barry stop him? (Yes. Yes he will.)

PEDRO THE PIGEON

MAD SCIENTIST

Pedro is a very fat pigeon who is also a TOTALLY MAD SCIENTIST! He creates crazy inventions in his secret lab to help Commander Zoom take over the world. He's the one who figured out how to bring cupcakes to life! His experiments often go wrong, but that just makes them MORE dangerous!

ROLAND T. RAT

THE SLEEPY SIDEKICK

Roland T. Rat is Commander Zoom's sidekick. Well, he's SUPPOSED to be. The problem is, Roland is ALWAYS falling asleep! Every time there's an important evil mission or a big battle, Roland has nodded off somewhere. He misses ALL the action! Zoom keeps him around anyway because... well, actually nobody knows why.
