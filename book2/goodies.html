BARRY

THE HERO

Barry is the CHILLEST capybara you'll ever meet. Nothing fazes him... not even a genius elephant trying to take over the world with terrifying inventions. Barry's secret weapon? Being SO relaxed that it drives the elephant absolutely CRAZY.

NOODLE

BARRY'S TANGLED FRIEND

Noodle is a really, REALLY long snake. So long that he's always getting himself tangled up in knots! This time, Noodle's tangly skills might actually come in handy - imagine trying to tie up an angry elephant! He means well and tries to help Barry, but usually ends up wrapped around himself.

POPSICLE

THE THREE-HAT PENGUIN

Popsicle is back with ALL THREE HATS ready for action! This time, his hat-stacking skills might be the key to victory. Can a penguin with three hats outsmart a genius elephant? Popsicle thinks so. Nobody knows why, but he's very confident about it.

TURBO

THE "FASTEST" SNAIL ALIVE

Turbo is STILL convinced he's the fastest creature alive. He's already planning to race the elephant. "Elephants are BIG but SLOW," says Turbo, who is literally a snail. His determination is unmatched, even if his speed isn't!

PROFESSOR FOXY

THE GENIUS INVENTOR

Professor Foxy has met her match! The Destructive Elephant is ALSO a genius scientist. Now it's a battle of brains - Foxy's inventions vs. the elephant's inventions! She's already working on an anti-elephant-tantrum device in her lab. Will it work? She's about 73% sure!

GERTRUDE THE COW

THE GRATEFUL ESCAPEE

Gertrude was once a secret experiment in The Destructive Elephant's underground lab — which is why her tail puffed up to the size of a small cloud and her horns grew MASSIVELY wide. She escaped and has been jolly ever since! Gertrude loves absolutely EVERYONE... except The Destructive Elephant. She is deeply, endlessly grateful to Barry and the gang for helping her stay free, and she will remind them of this every single day.
