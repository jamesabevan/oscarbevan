THE DESTRUCTIVE ELEPHANT

THE VILLAIN

The angriest genius you'll ever meet! This ENORMOUS elephant builds incredible (and terrible) inventions to take over the world. Don't make him angry... actually, he's ALWAYS angry! With his cracked tusks, steam-blasting ears, and a lab coat full of dangerous experiments, he's the most destructive villain Barry has ever faced. His latest invention? You DON'T want to know!

TOTTER THE TORTOISE

THE SIDEKICK (SORT OF)

Totter is The Destructive Elephant's official genius inventor sidekick — and his inventions are MIND-BLOWING. Better than anything Professor Foxy or Pedro could ever dream up. The only problem? He. Is. So. Slow. By the time Totter finishes building his incredible contraption, everyone has gone home for tea. He also has a habit of slipping upside-down and getting absolutely stuck, legs wiggling in the air, which makes the Elephant absolutely FURIOUS. Off duty, Totter completely forgets he's supposed to be a baddie. He's extremely friendly, waves at everyone, and regularly takes his whole family out for lovely days out.
